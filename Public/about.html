About Us

Licensed therapists ready to help
100% Online

There was a time when people thought of psychological therapy as a taboo. Fortunately, things changed and help was offered to everyone that needed it and everyone that asked for it.
Now, life got a little faster, we grew a little distant, we forgot to stop for a minute a look inside ourselves. We forgot about ourselves while trying to catch up on life, trying to be there for everyone. We got stressed chasing our dreams, achieve our goals, fighting for what we believe is destined for us and somewhere along the way we all fell down, we all felt caged, we forced ourselves to see though the jungle but we just couldn’t, we were stressed, we were somewhat depressed and just felt lost. And the thing is we didn't want to slow down because the one second you put your guard down we felt like things slipped from our fingers. And all this time you didn’t notice that you forgot to take care of you and your mind. And then you discovered us!
We are here to help you along the way in the easiest possible way, we are here for you day and night, to listen to you, to support you, to guide you, to help you get pass your stress, your inner struggles, your depression, your obsession, and even if you need someone just to talk to...
We are HERE!
We are here at the tip of your fingers, you just need to reach for us, chat with us online, make a call or feel free to make a video call. Don’t let your day to day routine imprison your mind, free yourself get started and join our Mindify family!

Our counselors

Mindify offers access to licensed, trained, experienced, and accredited psychologists (PhD / PsyD), marriage and family
therapists (LMFT), clinical social workers (LCSW / LMSW), and board licensed professional counselors (LPC).
